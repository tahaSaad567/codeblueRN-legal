Child Safety Standards

Commitment by Code Blue RN App

At Code Blue RN, we are fully committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) across our platform.

Our Commitments

  • Strict prohibition of any content related to child sexual abuse or exploitation.
  • Automated and manual monitoring of reported content.
  • Immediate removal of harmful content and permanent banning of violating accounts.
  • Cooperation with law enforcement when required.
  • Easy in-app reporting system for users to flag inappropriate content.
